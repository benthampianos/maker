Contact

Send me an email if you have any questions. You will get an automatic response immediately and I will aim to reply within 3 working days. If you are interested in booking my services, please provide as many details as possible:

  • The service you require;
  • Dates & times that work for you - please check my availability and location here before enquiring about a booking;
  • The type of piano (grand, upright, square, etc.);
  • The make of the piano;
  • The year of the piano if known;
  • The type of action if known (standard, overdamper, special patent, etc.);
  • Any known prior repairs.

Biography

I'm Yvonne Cheng, a piano tuner and technician based in the UK. I completed my training at The Piano Technology School UK in January 2025, supplemented by work experience at Piano Restorations Limited.

I am currently continuing my training with former Bechstein & Steinway technician Rolf Dragstrar. I'm also being mentored by early-keyboards specialist Cesar Hernandez on historical-instruments tuning & maintenance.

When not tuning and repairing pianos, I enjoy performing chamber music with my ensemble.
